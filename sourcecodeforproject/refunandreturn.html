Return and Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy

If a customer changes their mind and wants to cancel the order, then they have to contact our Customer Care executive. The cancellation will not be valid until it is confirmed by our respective department.

Cancellation requests will be considered only if the product is not dispatched. Once the order is shipped, customer cannot cancel the order.

If the customer has placed an order through Online Payment and cancels the order, then we will process for a refund within 15 working days.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most unopened and unused products in their original packaging. Some products may have different return policies as specified on the product page.

Conditions for Returns:

  • Product must be in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Certain products like seeds and perishables are non-returnable
  • Products damaged due to customer misuse are not eligible for return

Return Process:

  1. Contact our customer service to initiate a return
  2. Receive return authorization and instructions
  3. Pack the item securely and ship it back to us
  4. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund

Refund Policy

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 15 business days after we receive and inspect your return. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Refund Methods:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-15 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 business days

Note: During peak seasons, refund processing may take longer. You will receive an email notification once your refund has been processed.

Shipping Policy

We aim to process and ship orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected.

Shipping Options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Local Pickup: Available at select locations

Shipping Restrictions: Some agricultural products may have shipping restrictions to certain regions due to local regulations. We'll notify you if your order is affected.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with return authorization and instructions.

What if I receive a damaged product?

If you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.

Can I return opened agricultural chemicals?

For safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened agricultural chemicals, pesticides, or herbicides. Unopened products in their original sealed packaging may be returned within 30 days.

How long does a refund take to process?

Refunds typically take 5-15 business days to process after we receive your return, depending on your payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued.
