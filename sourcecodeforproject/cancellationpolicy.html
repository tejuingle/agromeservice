Order Cancellation Policy

General Cancellation Policy

At Agromeplus, we understand that sometimes you may need to cancel an order. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our customers and our business operations.

Important Note:

For agricultural products with short shelf lives (seeds, live plants, perishable items), cancellations must be requested within 2 hours of order placement to be eligible for full refund.

When can you cancel an order?

  • Before the order has been processed for shipping
  • Within 24 hours of placing the order (for non-perishable items)
  • If there will be a significant delay in shipping (we will notify you)

When can't you cancel an order?

  • After the order has been shipped
  • For custom or special order items
  • For perishable goods after the 2-hour cancellation window

Cancellation Process

Follow these steps to cancel your order:

1

2

Provide Details

3

Confirmation

Wait for our confirmation that your cancellation has been processed

4

Refund

If payment was made, refund will be processed within 5-15 business days

Note: If your order has already been shipped, you may need to follow our return process upon receiving the items.

Refund After Cancellation

If your cancellation request is approved, your refund will be processed based on your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 7-15 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: No refund needed as payment wasn't processed

Partial Cancellations

For orders with multiple items where only some items are cancelled, shipping fees may not be refunded if the remaining items don't qualify for free shipping.

Cancellation FAQs

How do I know if my order can still be cancelled?

Check your order status in "My Account" section or contact our customer service. Generally, if your order status is "Processing" it can still be cancelled. Once it changes to "Shipped", cancellation is no longer possible.

What if I need to cancel an order of agricultural chemicals?

For safety reasons, cancellations of agricultural chemicals must be requested within 1 hour of ordering. After this window, we cannot cancel these orders due to special handling requirements.

Will I be charged any fees for cancellation?

We don't charge cancellation fees, but payment gateway fees (if applicable) may not be refunded. For cash on delivery orders, there are no charges for cancellation.

Can I cancel an order placed with a discount coupon?

Yes, but the coupon will only be reinstated if the cancellation is made within the coupon's validity period. Expired coupons cannot be reused.

What if I cancel an order that was part of a bundle deal?

Bundle discounts will be recalculated based on the remaining items. If the remaining items no longer qualify for the bundle discount, the prices will be adjusted to regular prices.
